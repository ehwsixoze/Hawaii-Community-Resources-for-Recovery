Supporting Your Journey to Lasting Recovery Through Community Connections

Welcome to Your Resource Guide

This presentation provides a comprehensive overview of community resources available to individuals who have completed substance use disorder treatment in Hawaii. These resources are designed to support your continued recovery journey through housing, employment, financial assistance, peer support, and healthcare services.

Focus: Post-treatment community support services (not treatment centers or facilities)

Temporary Assistance for Needy Families (TANF/TAONF)
Provides monthly cash benefits to families for basic needs. TANF is for U.S. citizens, TAONF is Hawaii's state-funded program for non-citizens. Includes requirement for substance abuse treatment if needed.
Contact Information: Website: humanservices.hawaii.gov/bessd/tanf/
Apply: pais-benefits.dhs.hawaii.gov
Toll-free: 1-855-643-1643
Processing centers statewide
Eligibility Requirements: Hawaii resident; families with children under 18; gross income below 185% Federal Poverty Level; must participate in First-To-Work Program; seeking substance abuse treatment if determined necessary.

Contact Information: Website: humanservices.hawaii.gov/bessd/snap/

Toll-free: 1-855-643-1643
